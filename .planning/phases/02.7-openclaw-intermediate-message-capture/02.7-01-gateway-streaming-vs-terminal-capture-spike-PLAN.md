## Objective

Probe the real Gateway surface and write down what is actually available for intermediate assistant-message capture. Compare both acquisition routes:

1. true `/v1/chat/completions` streaming
2. terminal-body-only capture after the response completes

The output of this plan is a dated spike finding that locks the acquisition strategy for the rest of Phase 2.7.

## Must Be True

- The probe records whether streaming emits usable assistant text/events on the real shipped Gateway surface.
- The probe records what terminal-body capture can recover when the call is non-streaming.
- The results are written to `02.7-SPIKE-FINDINGS.md` with exact payload evidence, dates, and a decision: `streaming-primary` or `terminal-body-primary`.
- If streaming is rejected, the reason is explicit: unavailable, incomplete, unstable, or too hard to correlate with tool traffic.
